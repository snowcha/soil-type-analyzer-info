How to prep

You need: a glass jar with lid, water, and soil.

1

Add soil (remove rocks and debris).

2

Add water, more than the soil.

3

Close with the lid, shake well.

4

Leave it for 24 hours.

How to analyze

Using a photo
1

Take a photo of the prepped jar from the side, at eye level.

2

Slide the level bars to set each line.

Manual input
1

Toggle the top selector to Manual Input.

2

Enter each layer's thickness in cm or inch.

Either way, your soil type and tips appear under the photo.
